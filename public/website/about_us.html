Who We Are

VDocs is a health-tech startup dedicated to transforming how Indians manage their health information. We're building India's most trusted personal health record platform that puts patients in complete control of their medical data.

Our team combines expertise in healthcare, technology, and data security to create a solution that's both powerful and easy to use, while fully aligned with India's digital health vision.

Patient-First Approach

Every feature is designed with the patient's needs, privacy, and control as the top priority.

Our Journey

VDocs was founded with the belief that no patient should lose access to their health records or depend on scattered files. We aim to simplify healthcare for millions through a secure digital vault.

Did you know? 1 in 5 misdiagnoses happen due to lost or inaccessible medical reports. This reality inspired us to create a solution that ensures your health history is always available when you need it most.
